Services

Dr. Bindu Aravind's clinic stands committed to offering psychotherapy services designed to address various mental health problems as well as boost overall wellbeing. We tailor every service provided according to an individual client's individual needs while making certain each person receives compassionate, individualized care.

1 Individual Therapy

Individual therapy sessions offer patients a safe space in which to discuss issues that are personal to them, learn coping mechanisms and work toward an improved state of wellbeing in their life. Dr. Bindu utilises evidence-based approaches such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Mindfulness Based Therapy in her sessions in order to support clients:

  • Control Depression and Anxiety
  • Create Self Esteem and Confidence
  • Achieve Work Life Balance and Reduce Stress.

2 Couples and Relationship Counseling

A healthy relationship relies on communication, understanding and growth - three elements Dr. Bindu offers couples counseling services for in order to help strengthen these components by:

  • Recognizing and Addressing areas of Conflict to Improve Communication
  • Abilities and Build Emotional Trust and Strength

3 Family Counseling

Family dynamics have an enormous influence on an individual's mental wellbeing. Our counseling for families seeks to encourage healthy interactions while managing conflict more successfully so as to form long-lasting, supportive bonds among members of a household. Our services for family counselling include:

  • Parental Guidance Family Changes (e.g. Relocation or Divorce),
  • Generation Gaps and Divergent Views

4 Stress & Anxiety Management

Modern life can be very demanding on us all - successfully managing stressors is essential to staying well and Dr Bindu offers comprehensive anxiety and stress treatment services to address them effectively for wellbeing. Dr. Bindu offers these treatments:

  • Recognizing stressors and developing stress-reducing strategies
  • Offering mindfulness and relaxation practices.

5Trauma and PTSD Counseling

Healing after trauma is often difficult. Dr. Bindu offers trauma-focused treatments designed to assist her clients in processing and recovering from trauma using methods like:

  • Trauma-informed CBT
  • Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR)
  • Learning how to ground oneself while developing coping skills mes

6 Grief Counseling

Grief is an understandable response to loss; however, its management can be daunting. Bindu, Dr. Bindu provides supportive grief counsel

  • Help clients understand and express their emotions around loss
  • provide strategies for dealing with it
  • assist them to find meaning and balance after experiencing this tragedy
